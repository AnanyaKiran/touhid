About

Our Academies - Nurturing Talents, Instilling Confidence

At Touhid Education Society (TES), we believe every child has unique talents. Our endeavor is to recognize and hone their abilities, providing opportunities for students to excel in their areas of interest.

UNITY SPORTS ACADEMY

Established in 2019, our aim is to cultivate a lifelong love for sports while instilling the values of perseverance, fair play, and resilience in every student.

Sports is an integral part of the curriculum. We have a fully equipped Sports Room and a qualified, dedicated and experienced Sports Teacher. Through skill-building sessions and hands-on practice, our students are encouraged to try a variety of sports such as throwball, table tennis, volleyball, carrom, and more.

Regular training sessions are conducted to ensure students build physical fitness, discipline, and teamwork. These sessions are tailored to help children enhance their skills and grow in confidence, preparing them for competitive opportunities at various levels.

Our students have represented the school at district, regional, and even state-level competitions. Their accomplishments stand as a testament to the rigorous training, mentorship, and encouragement provided by the academy.

Proud Moments
  • Mohammed Ismail Mulla qualified for State-level Walkathon (5 Km) in Nov 2024.
  • Tasmiya won 1st Place in 100m and 200m sprints.
  • Arshiya Sayed achieved 3rd Prize in 100m & 400m runs at Zonal and Taluka levels.

UNITY ROBOTIC ACADEMY

Established in collaboration with TechnoRobo in 2017, this was a first-of-its-kind initiative in the district of Karwar. The academy is designed to equip young learners with advanced knowledge and practical skills in robotics and technology.

Guided by qualified faculty, our facility features a modern robotics lab. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on experience, allowing students to design and build robots and program them for various tasks. Students follow a simplified engineering process built around five essential steps: Imagine, Ask, Plan, Create, and Improve. This structured approach helps students enhance their creativity, problem-solving abilities, and teamwork while developing a solid understanding of robotics and engineering.

The academy successfully conducted two Robotic Exhibitions in 2018 and 2019. In 2024, our School secured 3rd Prize in Science Drama competition on Artificial Intelligence at State Level.

UNITY COMPUTER ACADEMY

Established in 1994, the Academy has been at the forefront of integrating technology into education, making computers an integral part of the curriculum for students as young as kindergarten and extending up to Degree level.

The academy offers hands-on training in essential software and its applications. While foundational skills like Computer Operations and MS Office are taught, we also offer an exclusive In-House Certified Program in advanced software like Adobe Photoshop.

We have two computer labs, one dedicated to school students and the other for college-level learners. These labs are well-equipped with 20 computers and staffed by highly qualified professionals who provide personalized attention and quality training.

UNITY MUSIC ACADEMY

Established in 2021, the academy provides students with an opportunity to explore the world of music through well-structured programs tailored to their interests.

Classical Section

Our Classical Section honors India's rich musical heritage, with a special focus on the Rudraveena. This ancient and rare instrument, on the verge of extinction, is taught with the support of the nonprofit organization Anam Prem, dedicated to preserving this cultural treasure.

Western Section

For those drawn to modern music, our Western Section offers professional training in Piano, Drums, and Guitar. These courses are led by a highly qualified music teacher with a passion for developing talent and creativity.

The Academy has dedicated rooms equipped with musical instruments for both Classical and Western music programs. Students have the freedom to choose their preferred section or explore both, allowing them to create a musical journey that resonates with their passion.
