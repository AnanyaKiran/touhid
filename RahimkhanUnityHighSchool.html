About

Rahimkhan Unity High School

Rahimkhan Unity High School (RUHS), commonly referred to as Unity High School was established in 1953. Located in Karwar, Karnataka, RUHS is a proud minority, co-educational, and government-aided school. It has earned a reputation for maintaining high academic standards and fostering a culture of holistic development. Our dedicated and highly qualified faculty ensure that all students receive the best guidance to excel in both academics and extracurricular pursuits.

Our Infrastructure

The school offers a well-equipped campus to enhance the learning experience:

  • Smart Classrooms powered by Educom.
  • Specialized Facilities: Chemistry laboratory, Computer Lab, and a Library-cum-Reading Room housing over 5000 books.
Curriculum & Activities

Fostering growth through engaging and diverse activities.

  • Sports & Extracurriculars: Opportunities for sports, elocution, debates, quizzes, exhibitions, and interschool/state-level competitions.
  • Active Clubs: Language Club, Science Club, Maths Club, Eco Club, Literary Club, and Sports Club foster diverse skills.
  • Leadership & Confidence Building: Activities and competitions develop life skills and unlock potential.
Academic Excellence

Shaping Future Leaders

RUHS has a consistent record of strong academic performance, particularly in Class X Board Examinations. Our disciplined approach, ethical values, and innovative teaching ensure a well-rounded learning experience.

Innovative Features

Pioneering New Learning Techniques

  • Clobas Integration: A platform bridging school and parents with updates on assignments, attendance, results, events, and more.
  • Techno ROBO: A district-first initiative incorporating robotics into the curriculum, integrating STEM for hands-on problem-solving and innovation.
Mode of Instruction

English and Urdu Medium Instruction

RUHS offers both English and Urdu medium instruction from Class VI to Class X, affiliated with the Karnataka State Board.

  • Urdu Medium:
    • First Language: Urdu
    • Second Language: Kannada
    • Third Language: English
  • English Medium:
    • First Language: English or Urdu
    • Second Language: Kannada
    • Third Language: Hindi (for First Language English) or English (for First Language Urdu)
