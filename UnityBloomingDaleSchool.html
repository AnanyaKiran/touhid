About

Unity Blooming Dale School

UBDS was established in 2003 with a mission to provide quality education at an affordable cost. It is the first primary school in North Karnataka to earn the prestigious ISO 9001:2008 certification.

The school provides a holistic learning experience by integrating modern educational techniques with a strong foundation in academics. Our curriculum encourages creativity, personal growth, and discipline, preparing students to excel academically and in life.

Why Choose Us
Comprehensive Learning

UBDS prepares students for today’s competitive world by focusing on an approach to academics, sports, creativity, computer literacy, and moral education.

Qualified Faculty

Our team of highly qualified, professional, and dedicated educators employ innovative teaching techniques to provide personal attention and guidance to each student.

Computer Lab

The academy provides hands-on training in foundational and advanced software, including Adobe Photoshop, using well-equipped labs and qualified instructors.

Library

Students have access to our vast collection of books, including general knowledge, stories, magazines, and articles, thereby encouraging curiosity.

More facilities
Academic Excellence

UBDS follows a rigorous academic monitoring system with monthly tests and semester exams conducted for students from Std 1 -Std 5. Our well-structured curriculum is designed to ensure students excel in both academics and extracurricular pursuits.

Parental Involvement

Parents are an integral part of a child's education. Regular parent-teacher meetings ensure a collaborative approach to discussing students' academic progress and overall behavior.

Creative Development

At UBDS, we believe every child is a bundle of potential. Teachers identify and nurture individual talents, encouraging students to participate in a wide range of in-school and inter-school competitions.

Affiliation and Curriculum

Empowering Girls for a Brighter Future

Students from UBDS have the opportunity to integrate with Rahimkhan Unity High School from Class VI to Class X.

Medium of Instruction: English

Unity Blooming Dale School is committed to nurturing an environment where children can thrive academically, creatively, and personally, laying a strong foundation for their future.
