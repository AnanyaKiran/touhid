About

Ameerbi Rahimkhan PU College of Commerce

ARPUC was established in 2012 with a mission to empower women through quality education. In 2022, the college evolved to embrace co-education, encouraging an inclusive environment where students from diverse backgrounds come together to learn, grow, and achieve their goals. Built on the values of integrity, inclusivity, and innovation, we ensure that every student receives a well-rounded learning experience that prepares them for the challenges of the modern world.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

To support our students in their academic and personal growth, we offer a range of top-notch facilities.

Qualified and Dedicated Staff

Our team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members are dedicated to nurturing the potential of every student. With a passion for teaching and mentorship, they guide students toward academic success and personal development.

Library

Our well-stocked library provides students with access to an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources. The serene environment encourages focused study and research.

Computer Lab

Equipped with the latest technology and high-speed internet, our computer lab enables students to enhance their technical skills and stay updated with the advancements in commerce and related fields.

Holistic Development

All-Round Student Growth

At ARPUC, education goes beyond textbooks. We emphasize the all-round development of our students by encouraging participation in extracurricular activities, sports, and community service. This balanced approach helps students build confidence, leadership skills, and a sense of social responsibility.
