Our President's Message

“Welcome! We are delighted to invite you to explore our Group of Institutions and participate in our journey of nurturing young minds and shaping their future. We firmly believe, that 'No child should be deprived of receiving education due to lack of funds and resources.'”

I am privileged to share our story, how from a tiny sapling, our Unity Group of Institutions have grown into a mighty tree. Sky is the limit for us, and we aspire to further grow! In June of 1953, when Rahimkhan Unity High School (RUHS) was founded by my late father, Rahimkhan Omerkhan, I was only 12 years old and its first student. Since then, I have watched its growth, its trials and tribulations, its success and failures from the closest quarters, our own home. My mother stood by my father’s every move and provided unquestionable support.

In 1977, upon the sad demise of my mother, my father sheltered a poor family of five in his own home and looked after their every need, without any outside support. As the number of children grew, my father had the vision to realize that the support from family may not be lasting forever, due to fluctuations and vagaries of life, and hence decided to take these children under the umbrella of Touhid Education Society (TES). We named it as Mother's Home, inspired by the well-known similar home in Mumbai known as 'Baap nu ghar' (Father's Home).

Mother's Home initially had about 50 girls, but 13 of them had to be excluded owing to the Department's strict regulations. Thus, Qudsia Banu Unity Women's Home was born. Today, this is a 2-storeyed building with 83 under-privileged girls. All 120 girls in both homes are provided with full amenities and are enrolled in the Unity Group of Institutions.

After a long innings of 39 years as President of TES and its Unity Group of Institutions, I have stepped down, making way for Vice-President, Dr. Fazila Patankar, to take over as the new President. With over 17 years of experience in Public Health, I am confident that she, along with TES Management, will deal squarely with all the challenges and further elevate the status of our Institutions to make them the most sought-after in the City of Karwar.
- Etibar Shah R Khan, President Emeritus

About Us

Touhid Education Society

Touhid Education Society (TES) was founded in 1953 by late, Mr. Rahimkhan Omerkhan. A great visionary, he started with only five classes (Std 5th to 9th). We are located in the heart of Karwar city at Unity Educational Complex, Kodibag, Karwar -581 303, Karnataka, INDIA. Since then, TES has evolved into a major center of academic excellence, encompassing eight institutions:

  • 1953: Rahimkhan Unity High School
  • 1959: Unity Primary School
  • 1975: Unity Phool Bun (Kindergarten)
  • 1978: Mother’s Home (Girls Orphanage)
  • 2003: Unity Bloomingdale School
  • 2012: Ameerbi Rahimkhan Junior College for Women
  • 2014: Feroza Hasham Degree College of Commerce for Women
  • 2019: Qudsia Banu Unity Women’s Home (Women’s Home)
