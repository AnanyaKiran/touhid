About

"My Unity" Initiative

"My Unity" is an initiative under the Touhid Education Society, dedicated to fostering a sense of community and collaboration. We believe in the power of unity to create positive change and uplift marginalized groups, ensuring everyone has access to quality education and opportunities.

Our mission is to build bridges between diverse communities, enabling them to work together towards common goals. We focus on empowerment through education and skill development, providing individuals with the tools they need to succeed.

Unity Talks

Inspired by Ted Talks, Unity Talks are short talks, covering a range of subjects, with the intention to inspire, motivate and guide our students, the local youth, and the community at large.

The speakers are from different fields and include but not limited to our alumni, professional life coaches, educational experts, businessman, and motivational speakers. This is a platform to present and share ideas and encourage discussion and debate. We passionately believe in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives and ultimately, the World.
